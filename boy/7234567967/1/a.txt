Being the middle kid of the family comes with its own set of struggles. Your life changes the day your younger sibling comes, as then you are no longer the pampered younger one, and somewhere you start feeling like nobody cares to notice you. You get all the responsibilities of being the older one and, all the restrictions of being the younger one too. But it seems the couples today are content with one or two kids, gradually depleting the concept of having a middle child. In fact, we might have entered an era of extinction of the middle child.

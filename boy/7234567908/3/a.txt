The book follows the 26-year-old new mother Anna Johnson who has been freshly orphaned by her parents suicides. Grappling with the magnanimity of motherhood, at the same time being grief-stricken, Anna seems adrift and a bit disconnected with reality. When Anna gets a mysterious card during her mothers first death anniversary, she is convinced of her doubts regarding her parents death. When Anna is deemed delusional and cast aside, retired detective Murray Mackenzie agrees to help her. But as the investigations begin, Anna starts getting anonymous threats and she must choose between either the truth or her babys safety.

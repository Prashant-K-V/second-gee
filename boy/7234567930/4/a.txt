Other than meat, lentils are considered one of the richest sources of protein. And the goodness of lentils doesnt end there - they pack a hefty dose of fibre and carbohydrates as well. Having a bowl of lentils every day at lunch can do wonders for your body. And, the best bit about lentils is the variety you get them in. There are just endless options of the delicious goodness that is bound to keep you healthy and your taste buds happy.

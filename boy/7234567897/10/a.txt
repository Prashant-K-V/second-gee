The mindset that it is only the mothers responsibility to raise the child and the father should only be the breadwinner of the family, needs to change. It is important that the Paternity Benefit Bill sees the light of the day as it is essential for a father to be present along with new mother to provide her with much-needed care and support, both during and after pregnancy. Partners need to be very understanding and gentle with their wives and experience this journey along with them.

The play revolves around the lives of Susan and David--an Indian origin couple--who are living in the US. It probes deeper into the mental condition of Susan -- the protagonist of the play-- which worsens after the delivery of her first child. She goes into depression and starts hearing voices of a physician from the 19 th century named Dr Marce. It focuses on her whole journey, including her apprehensions and fears about motherhood and the myths attached to it.

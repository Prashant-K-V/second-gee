 A book lover reads a lot of books, undoubtedly. And when you read a lot, you come across many characters and hence, youre also able to relate to people from different spheres of life. This makes a reader more empathetic towards fellow humans and other animals, because they are more likely to understand their plight than the ones who dont read. This can be linked to the neurological term theory of mind, according to which, Literary fictionoften described as narratives that focus on in-depth portrayals of subjects inner feelings and thoughts can be linked to theory of mind processes, especially those that are involved in the understanding or simulation of the effective characteristics of the subjects.

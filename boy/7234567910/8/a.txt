If youre having trouble sleeping at night, try reading regularly before going to bed. Chances are, you will have a more sound sleep than youve had previously, and actually, also get up with a fresher, more cheerful and purposeful mood. Neuroscientist Anne-Marie Chang says that a book can help you relax sooner than looking at a screen. After all, books have been known to cure insomnia.

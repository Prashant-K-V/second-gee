It is scientifically proven that people who read a lot have complex brains that work at a slightly higher level than those who dont. This fact was proven by Ken Pugh at Yale University. Well, the benefits of a good memory are pretty much self-explanatory. Book lovers have nothing to worry about remembering dates and important incidents from past. Their super-strong brain helps them just fine. According to a study made by the Archives of Neurology in Berkeley, reading regularly from a young age can even help counter Alzheimers Disease

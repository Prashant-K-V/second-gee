 Set in Prague and Zurich, this book shows how adultery is viewed by different people. Tom and Tereza are married but Tom views love and sex as separate so he womanises despite his marriage. His wife Tereza doesnt have a very positive image of her body and worries that she is just another body to her husband. However their marriage evolves throughout their life. We also see the perspective of one of Tom lovers and her other relationship.

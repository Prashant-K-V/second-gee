Your home is who you are. It reflects your personality and needs. It exemplifies YOU. Color and light are the two most important components that make a home. It sets the tone and the character of your house. Each color says something about the mood of the room, and you.

Color and light not only influence human emotions, they are also important components of changing the look or feel of a room. A coat of paint and the right lighting can dramatically change interiors, camouflage design problems and other defects. It can improve moods and reduce negative behavior, it can increase or decrease blood pressure, it can improve concentration and productivity, and it can even increase appetite.

Right from the time your daughter scored less, or she fell and got bruised, you feel like a failure for letting that happen. You put your heart and soul in your parenting that you often cant hide your disappointments and elation. But in the bargain, you forget taking care of your emotions and yourself, in general. There are times when you realise it is noon and you havent had the time to brush your teeth or its been three days you havent combed your hair.

Skandamata is a face of Maa Durga that is worshipped on the fifth day. This form of the Goddess is known for demolishing the demons and establishing peace in the universe. She is dressed in a blue sari and on this day and the Upang Lalitha Gauri Vrata is observed. Devotees are supposed to wear grey on this day. Snehal Khatri, a professional, said, “As per the Hindu mythology, Goddess along with her son Kartikeya and a troupe of angels fight with the demons on this day. This day also signifies power and strength.Shades of grey are worn on this day which signifies black colour of demons and white colour of angels.“

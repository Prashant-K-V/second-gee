Chandraghanta is the third appearance of the goddess who symbolizes peace, serenity, beauty and bravery in ones life. As per the symbolic representation of peace, she is adorned in a white sari on the third day of Navratri. Yellow is the colour of the day for devotees. Rima Patil, an architect, said, “We keep Gauri vrata or fast for the Goddess on this day.Sindoor Tritiya or Sowbhagya Teej is observed on this day where we wear yellow coloured saris. We pray for our husbands prosperity, good health and longlasting marriage.“

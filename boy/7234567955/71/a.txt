Siddhidatri is the form of Maa Durga for the ninth day of Navratri and is known to have great supernatural powers. Siddhidatri personifies all the eight siddhis (vedas or supernatural powers). As per Hindu scriptures, Lord Shiva worshipped Siddhidatri and was gifted with all the siddhis. This Goddess is dressed in purple attire and Maha Navami puja as well as Kanya puja is performed on this day. Sky blue is the colour of the day for all the enthusiastic devotees on this day.
